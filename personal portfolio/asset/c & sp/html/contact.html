Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me, project processes, and collaboration details

What is your typical project process?

My process typically involves: 1) Initial consultation to understand your needs, 2) Project proposal with timeline and cost estimate, 3) Agreement and deposit, 4) Design/development phase with regular updates, 5) Review and revisions, 6) Final delivery and launch, 7) Post-launch support. The exact process may vary based on project type and complexity.

What are your rates and pricing structure?

I offer both project-based pricing and hourly rates depending on the scope of work. For well-defined projects, I provide fixed quotes. For ongoing work or projects with evolving requirements, I work on an hourly basis ($50-75/hr depending on complexity). Detailed pricing for specific services is available on my Services page. All projects begin with a free consultation to determine scope and provide an accurate quote.

What is your average project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project complexity: Basic websites (2-4 weeks), Web applications (4-12 weeks), Brand identity projects (3-6 weeks), Platform development (8-20 weeks). During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements. I prioritize quality and thoroughness while working efficiently to meet agreed deadlines.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. I'm experienced in remote collaboration across different time zones (primarily WAT/UTC+1). I use tools like Zoom, Slack, and project management platforms to ensure seamless communication. I'm flexible with meeting times and can accommodate most time zones for consultation calls.

What forms of payment do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum). For projects over $1,000, I typically require a 30-50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. For larger projects, I can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment terms are clearly outlined in the project agreement before work begins.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages for websites and applications. These typically include security updates, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and minor content updates. Maintenance plans start at $50/month depending on the complexity of the project. All projects include a warranty period (typically 30 days) after launch during which I fix any issues free of charge.
