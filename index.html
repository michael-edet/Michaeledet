About Me

My Journey

I'm Michael Edet, a passionate digital creator with expertise spanning graphic design, video editing, web development, and cybersecurity. With over 5 years of hands-on experience, I've honed my skills across multiple disciplines to become a versatile digital professional.

My journey began in graphic design and video editing, where I developed a keen eye for aesthetics and visual storytelling. This foundation led me to explore programming, where I discovered the power of combining creative design with technical implementation.

Currently, I'm pursuing a BSc. in Mathematics & Computer Science at the National Open University of Nigeria, Uyo Study Center. This academic pursuit complements my practical experience, providing me with a strong theoretical foundation in computing principles.
